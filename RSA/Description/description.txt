Fractured Kingdom
Category: Cryptography

The royal court trusted their “unbreakable” RSA encryption to safeguard a message that could topple an empire. But arrogance breeds mistakes. The modulus N you’ve intercepted looks formidable… yet whispers say its two prime pillars aren’t as far apart as they should be.

The ciphertext lies before you, daring you to break centuries of trust in a single stroke. Can you find the hidden cracks in the crown’s defenses and reveal the secret they tried to bury?
